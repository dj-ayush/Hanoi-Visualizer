How to Play

The objective is to move all disks from the left tower to the right tower.

Rules:

  • Only one disk can be moved at a time
  • A disk can only be placed on top of a larger disk or an empty tower
  • You can click on a tower to pick up or place a disk

Controls:

  • Click on a tower to select/deselect disks
  • Arrow keys to move between towers
  • Space/Enter to pick up/place disks
